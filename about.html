About

I am currently a third-year student interested in the structural engineering, design and construction of infrastructure. I am pursuing a major in Civil Engineering and working towards an Engineering Business Minor through the Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering and Rotman School of Management. Last summer I assisted MASc and Ph.D. students in the Structural Testing Labs where I was able to develop practical experience and see how some structural components work. This experience strengthened my interest in structural engineering and left me with an eagerness to learn more.
