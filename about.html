About

I am an undergraduate engineering student interested in the structural engineering, design and construction of infrastructure. I am pursuing a major in Civil Engineering and working towards an Engineering Business Minor through the Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering and Rotman School of Management. I am currently working as a co-op student in the Permit Delivery Office of the Engineering Services department at Toronto Hydro.

During my undergraduate studies, I assisted MASc and Ph.D. students in the Structural Testing Labs, where I was able to develop practical experience and see how some structural components work. This experience strengthened my interest in structural engineering and left me with an eagerness to learn more. Since then, I have joined the University of Toronto Seismic Design Team, where we design and construct a model tower to participate in the annual EERI seismic design competition.
