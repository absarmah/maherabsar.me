Transport 1 Position Paper

This short position paper was written for an Introduction to Transportation Systems course during my third year of undergraduate studies. The course introduces the basics of transportation engineering, transportation economics, planning, geometric design and the impacts of transportation engineering.

The paper required to establish a clear stance on a modern transportation issue. I choose to demonstrate how automated vehicles can be a viable solution to a major transportation problem of congestion in the urban environment. The report provides explanation of engineered strategies that automated vehicles can use to assist in mitigating road congestion. The strategies provided are supported with numerous academic studies.

The 500-word limit on this paper required being concise and informative. The short length off the paper has allowed me to communicate the impact of automated vehicles on the road effectively without being extensive. Summarizing the technical systems in place that support my argument of the positive impacts of automated vehicles allowed me to utilize necessary communication skills needed for the engineering profession.
